Classic Homemade Lasagna

Description

Classic Homemade Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, made with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey mozzarella. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this hearty dish brings a delicious Italian flavor right to your table.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) tomato sauce
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps to Make It:

  1. Cook the meat sauce
    • Brown ground beef, sausage, onion, and garlic.
    • Add crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water.
    • Season with sugar, basil, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and parsley.
    • Simmer for about 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the noodles
    • Boil lasagna noodles in salted water.
    • Drain and lay flat to prevent sticking.
  3. Mix the cheese filling
    • Combine ricotta cheese, egg, parsley, and salt.
    • Stir until smooth.
  4. Assemble the lasagna
    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread meat sauce on the bottom of the baking dish.
    • Layer noodles, ricotta, sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
    • Repeat and finish with cheese on top.
  5. Bake
    • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove foil and bake another 25 minutes until golden.
  6. Rest and serve
    • Let lasagna rest for 15 minutes before slicing.
    • Serve warm and enjoy!
